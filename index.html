 10. CONTROLLS FOR DO-NOT-TRACK FEATURES.

 

 Most Web browsers and some mobile operating systems and mobile applications include Do-Not-Track (‘DNT’) features or settings you can activate to signal your privacy preference not to have data about your online browsing activities monitored and collected. At this stage, no uniform technology standard for recognizing and implementing DNT signals has been finalized. As such, we do not currently respond to DNT browser signals or any other mechanism that automatically communicates your choice not to be tracked online. If a standard for online tracking is adopted we must follow in the future, we will inform you about that practice in a revised version of this privacy notice.
